About

I design for real-world performance

Most training tells people what to do. I build learning experiences that make them able to do it — under pressure, in regulated environments, with high stakes and diverse learner populations.

My background spans three industries where getting it wrong costs something: financial services (Fidelity, MetLife, Raymond James), healthcare (Moffitt Cancer Center), and adult workforce education. I've designed from scratch without templates, built compliance-ready programs, and reached learners across military, immigrant, and professional populations.

What makes my approach different: I'm multilingual, AI-fluent, and I understand the learner who didn't grow up in the system the training was built for. That lens makes the work better for every learner, not just the edge cases.
